Privacy Policy / गोपनीयता नीति

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

At Daily Doha Wisdom (accessible from https://dailydohawisdom-6c5be.web.app), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Daily Doha Wisdom and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide sensitive personal information to access our spiritual couplets, articles, or poster generation tools. Any custom input data (such as custom quote text or font preferences) is processed client-side in your web browser and saved strictly inside your browser's localStorage.

2. Log Files & Analytics

Daily Doha Wisdom follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising Cookies

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to https://dailydohawisdom-6c5be.web.app and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. GDPR & CCPA Privacy Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to data access, rectification, erasure, and restriction of processing under applicable regulations.
